Our Team

Managing Director

Mr. Alex Aboagye-Akari is a Geologist by profession with more than fifteen years (15) work experience in geological, civil and project management. He has a Diploma in Geological Engineering and Master of Science (MSc) in Geological Engineering all from the University of Mines and Technology, Tarkwa. He is a Superintendent Geologist and served in management position with vast experience in drilling activities, project management, exploration geology, hydrogeology, resource geology and mine geology. He is also the trainer of trainees and in charge of safety standards of his subordinates. He is a highly skilled, efficient, effective and multifaceted manager.

Director of Operations and Technical Services

Mr. Maxwell Yao Akpene Klu is a Geologist by profession with over Seven (7) years’ experience in drilling, resource geology, exploration geology and mine geology. He has a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Geological Engineering from the University of Mines and Technology. He also holds Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from University of London, UK. He is experienced in contract arrangement, Natural Resources and Environmental Law. He is also well vexed in Company Law, Commercial Law, Ghana’s Legal System and Constitutional Law. He is a team player and a goal getter with excellent oral and written communication. He is developmental oriented and an advocate of corporate social responsibility by companies to advance the development of host communities and beyond.

Head of Accounting and Finance

Mr. Prince Amenya holds HND in Accountancy from Ho Polytechnic and BSc.in Accounting from the Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (GIMPA). He also holds Masters in Business Administration (MBA) in Finance from the University of Ghana, Legon. He has over twenty (20) years’ experience in Accounting and Finance Management in both Private and Public institutions and at management levels. His role in our management team is to provide financial strategy and to manage the accounting function of Epicenter Co. Ltd. He provides excellent professional, independent and honest Financial, Accounting, Taxation and Managerial Services for the realization of our corporate goals and vision.

Head of Administration, HR & Clients Relations

Mr. Augustine Annan holds BSc. in Human Resource Management (HR). He has over eight (8) years’ experience in human resource management in both Private and Public institutions. His experience is in recruitment of employees, company dispute and grievance resolution, on the job training and capacity building of employees and employee motivation and promotions. His role in our management team is to facilitate the employment of highly qualified skilled professionals, formulating adequate controls for motivation and maintaining a peaceful and competitive working environment for the fulfilment of company’s goals vision.

Head of Marketing and Business Development

Mr. Saviour Komla Sobia Deikumah is a Chartered Accountant with Bachelor of Commerce, Bachelor of Laws and Final level of the Chartered institute of Taxation, Ghana. He is a member in Good Standing with the Institute of Chartered Accountants, Ghana with working experience at senior management level for more than eight (8) years across industries. Mr. Deikuma is an effective and efficient team leader with excellent oral and written communication skills and advanced financial modelling, marketing, legal research and report or proposal writing skills. His role in Epicenter is to develop modern and competitive marketing strategy for the company and set Business Development models in motion for the fulfilment of the goals and vision of the company.
