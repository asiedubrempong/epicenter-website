About Us

Our History

Epicenter Company limited was incorporated in May 2017 under Ghana’s Companies Code 1963 Act179, as a private company limited by shares to do the business of General Merchants, Building and Road Constructions, Civil Engineering and Drilling Services. The business was founded by two experienced Geologists and Civil Engineers as an indigenous local content mining, construction, and extractive sector logistics support service provider in Ghana. The founding directors, Mr. Alex Aboagye-Akari and Mr. Maxwell Yao Akpene Klu have more than 15 years working experience with the major mining license holders in Ghana, including Gold field Ghana limited.

We provide goods, equipment, construction, civil engineering, drilling and geological consultancy services for the growth of our clients’ operations. We also take up outsourced administrative and management support services including supply of loose tools and consumables, stationaries and corporate publications, repair and maintenance and field staff recruitment and training.

Our business development and client’s relationship team are always ready to engage with our clients and to tender in our proposals and expressions of interests as well as participating in any open competitive bidding process within the procurement frameworks of the industry. Our offices are currently located in the Obuasi and Tarkwa enclave closer to our target customers in the mining, drilling, oil and gas, construction and extractive business.

Our Vision

The vision of Epicenter Ghana Limited is to become the best local content indigenous company in the general supply of goods, drilling, construction and civil engineering sectors in Africa and beyond by setting the standard for superior quality, environmentally friendly services and the most sustainable financial performance.

Our Mission

We are incorporated as a local content business, offering construction, civil engineering, drilling and general supplies and logistical support services. We provide goods, works, supplies and consultancy services to government and private sector institutions including; district, municipal and metropolitan assemblies, ministries, departments and agencies of central government. Our private sector clients include mining, oil and gas and their allied companies.
